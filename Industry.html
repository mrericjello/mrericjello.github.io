The Web Industry

The Web Industry is a vast place of many different categories that overlap but have distinct differences. As a Web Designer, you must know basic areas of the Web Industry as your experiences in web design my fall into one or all of them.

eCommerce Online Business

Ecommerce used to be very expensive but is very cheap and hasmany options. Choose a business model before choosing the platform for which you want to build your site. Different needs between businesses means different models. Choose an interest: hobbies, passion, industry. Choose a product: Physical (Aquisition, storage and fulfillment with create, wholesale, Drop-Shipping or manufacture) vs. virtual (books, software, affiliate with publishing, programming, and recommendations). You can't have a website if you don't have anywhere to put it. Also, how are people suppose to find your website if it doesn't have an domain name? If someone is looking for your site, you need to have hosting services available to you to store all he necessary information for your website.

Web Technology Community

Collaboration Frameworks and Git

Technology is the product of developers working together. The web technology community has many outlets through which they collaborate. We can't grow in the technology industry if we don't share ideas. And more ideas from more people let us develop software through places like Github and online forums.

Developer Summits Tech Conventions and Summits

Technology conventions are hosted by some of the biggest industries in the tech community. There are always new and exciting software, hardware and robotics that come to the forefront of these conventions. Even sometimes military display technological inventions through their branches.

Local Communities Bit Wise, Fresno City, Fresno State

It is important as a developer to know of your local developer communities. In Fresno you have BitWise Industries that teach classes and host local dev fests. Fresno City College is a community college that teaches Computer Information Technology and Computer Science Courses. Also Fresno State University teaches courses in Computer Science and Information Systems. They take field trips to San Francisco and Silicon Valley for Dev Summits.
